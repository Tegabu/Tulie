Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an album?

Tap the '+ Album' icon on your profile page, then add members directly in the album.

Is my data really private?

Yes. All content shared within Tuli is End-to-End Encrypted, and we never sell your data to third parties. Your privacy is our top priority.

How do I report a problem?

Email support@tuliapp.com with a description of your issue, your username, and device details (e.g., iPhone 15, iOS 18).
