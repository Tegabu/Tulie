Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Check out our common questions below for immediate answers:

How do I create an album group chat?

You can Start a group chat by clicking the chat icon in the album you want to create a group chat of.

Is my data really private?

Yes. All content shared within Tuli is End-to-End Encrypted, and we never sell your data to third parties. Your privacy is our top priority.

How do I create a new album?

Tap the '+ Album' icon on your profile page, You can then add members directly in the album
