Why Tuli Exists

Reclaiming Digital Intimacy

In today’s hyper connected world, we’ve lost the art of private sharing. Social media has turned our most personal moments into public performances, measured by likes and driven by algorithms.

Tuli was born from a simple question: what if we could share our lives only with the people who truly matter? No pressure to perform, no fear of oversharing, just real moments shared with those who care.

With Tuli, you can create shared albums that bring people together—spaces where friends and family can add photos, videos, and memories, all wrapped around a group chat that keeps the conversation alive. Your personal feed is a reflection of your circle—only the people you follow and who follow you back—so every post feels familiar and genuine. And when you just want to connect one on one, Tuli’s direct messages make those quiet, personal conversations effortless.

We built Tuli to feel like a cozy living room conversation: intimate, secure, and meaningful. A place where your memories stay among the people who matter most, not lost in the noise of an endless feed.
